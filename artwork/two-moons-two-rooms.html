Two moons - Two rooms

1996

Painted and Patinated Brass, Copper and Aluminium

242 x 120 x 10 cms

"Two moons, Two rooms", captures the repetitive essence of daily existence. It's a reflection on the day after day after day cycle, a visual exploration of the ongoing nature of life. The variation in the colour of the square symbolises different days at my studio, adding to the narrative of continuity, the relentless march of time and the subtle shifts in my daily environment.
