Kelly 2

2000

Pastel on Paper

121 x 133 cms

I decided to enter a competition, choosing to portray Kelly's wife—an often overlooked figure in discussions about Ned Kelly. The piece took an unexpected turn, deviating from its initial portrait concept into a representation dominated by semi-curves. The connection between the project and my grandfather's blacksmithing profession became clear, inspiring many drawings and providing insight into the techniques involved.
