Remains of a Rock Wall

1991

Painted and Patinated Bronze, Brass and Copper

30 x 38 x 8 cms

The interplay of elements, such as the bluestone wall, sky, and celestial bodies like the Sun, Mercury, and Venus, reflects on the passage of time. The landscape and the gradual decay seen upon arriving in ??. The reference to the mechanical aspects, particularly in capturing the sun and space, highlights the practical challenges inherent in sculptural compositions. The use of bronze in the composition adds a tactile and enduring quality to the piece Earth and Sky.
