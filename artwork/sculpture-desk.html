Sculpture/Desk

1990

Painted and Polished Timber and Bronze

211 x 318.5 cms

I collaborated with a skilled wood worker to create a piece that blends art and function. Our collaboration resulted in a table design. The bronze cone, a focal point, has since aged over time. The pillar also unfolds to reveal a cabinet. This furniture-sculpture hybrid stands as a testament to post-Memphis design, it captures the essence of our engagement with Italian design upon our return from Italy.
