Nico

2025

Brass, powder coated aluminium, steel

225 x 74 x 40 cms

I'm not interested in portraiture as such, though this work may suggest otherwise. Nico is a composition of modernist symbols, shaped by a long engagement with art history and the visual language that evolves from it. It's also a return to ideas first explored in my early steel sculptures of the 1980s.
