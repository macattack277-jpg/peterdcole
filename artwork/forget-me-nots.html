Forget me nots

2025

Powder coated aluminium

Variable sizes 33-52 x 9 cms

The flowers came about through a number of directions. I like to make work that responds to what surrounds me at the time. Flowers have always been present in my life through Helen's practice and her ritual of keeping fresh flowers in the house, as well as her love of gardening. On another level, flowers represent peace. My hope is that, in looking at this work, one might momentarily step away from the wider noise of the world and focus instead on this small, quiet part of it.
