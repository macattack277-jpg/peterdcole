Two Trees

2008

Painted and Patinated Brass, Aluminium and Wood

60 x 40 x 15 cms

I used to point out a pair of trees to my sons during our drives to Melbourne. The trees always stood out to me, as they were not in the form of typical trees. I told my sons that if you were to draw a tree to look like that, it wouldn't resemble a typical tree, but in fact a square. It became central in this piece, creating a unique juxtaposition with the tree form and the moon hovering above.
