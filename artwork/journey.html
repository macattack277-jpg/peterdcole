Journey I, 2 and 3

2021

Painted and Patinated Brass, Bronze, Wood and Marble

59 x 100 x 8.5 cms

This work holds a special place for me, as it weaves through the landscape of memory and personal experiences. The first structure, resembling a diamond, appears flat but is actually a solid structure. The archetype of the house with its triangular roof, reminiscent of my time in India, stands out for me, offering a blend of home and being away from it.
