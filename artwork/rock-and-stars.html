Rock and Stars

1997

Painted and Patinated Brass, Bronze and Stainless Steel

2045 x 975 x 50 cms

This piece marks an early phase in my artistic journey – an exploration of landscape elements. You can sense a loose arrangement of elements, a less defined structure compared to my later works. The symbols, though recognisable, are scattered, and I felt the need to reorder them for better cohesion. It was a commissioned piece in the mid 80's by two photographers who had a studio in South Melbourne. The trend of commissioned pieces continued, it felt like the right time for such creative collaborations.
